Our Rafting Adventures

Discover our variety of white water rafting trips, designed for every experience level from beginners to advanced thrill-seekers. Each adventure offers unique challenges and breathtaking scenery.

Available Trips

Half-Day Adventure

Difficulty: Easy-Moderate (Class I-II Rapids)

Perfect for beginners and families, this 3-hour trip introduces you to the basics of rafting while enjoying gentle rapids and beautiful scenery. No experience necessary!

  • 3-hour guided tour
  • Suitable for ages 8+
  • Equipment and safety briefing included
  • Professional photos available

$89 per person

Full-Day Expedition

Difficulty: Moderate (Class II-III Rapids)

Our most popular trip! Experience a diverse range of rapids with increasing challenge throughout the day. Includes a riverside lunch and plenty of swimming opportunities.

  • 6-hour guided expedition
  • Suitable for ages 12+
  • Riverside lunch included
  • Swimming and cliff jumping spots

$159 per person

Extreme Rapids Challenge

Difficulty: Advanced (Class III-IV Rapids)

For experienced rafters seeking an adrenaline rush! Navigate through challenging white water, steep drops, and technical maneuvers on this heart-pumping adventure.

  • 5-hour intense rafting experience
  • Suitable for ages 16+
  • Previous rafting experience required
  • Includes post-trip celebration

$199 per person
